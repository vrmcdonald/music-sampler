The Internet, The Band

Syd and Matt Martians of The Internet performing in 2012. Photo by Incase on flickr.com.

The Internet, a Californian band of five artists, spans the soul, hip hop, R&B, funk and electronica genres. Combining catchy rhythms, soft vocals and “piercing” guitar solos, the band specializes in smooth and sensual music that’s easy to relax, dance or sing along to. Lead singer Syd serenades listeners alongside guitarist and vocalist Steve Lacy, keyboardist Matt Martians, Patrick Paige II on bass guitar and drummer Christopher Smith. If you’re in the market for love songs tenderly crooned by a woman to a woman, The Internet is for you.
